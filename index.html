Bluesky Quick Mute

Ready.

Add a word or phrase to your Bluesky muted words from the URL, for example:

https://colarusso.github.io/bluesky-mute/?mute=spoilers

You can also use this bookmarklet. Just drag Mute to your toolbar. Then you can highlight a word or phrase and click it to mute the selected text.

Use a Bluesky app password, not your main password. This page stores the identifier, app password, and service URL in localStorage on this browser because you asked for local storage. Do not host this on a domain you do not control.
